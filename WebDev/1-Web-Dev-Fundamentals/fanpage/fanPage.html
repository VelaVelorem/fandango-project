Founded on June 15, 1985, Studio Ghibli was headed by directors Hayao Miyazaki, Isao Takahata and producer Toshio Suzuki. Miyazaki and Takahata had already had long careers in Japanese film and television animation and had worked together on The Great Adventure of Horus, Prince of the Sun in 1968 and the Panda!
